Intro info

Three switches, top to bottom: fboss1, fboss2, fboss3.

Each physical connection (qsfp) is actually 4 physical ports: 1-4, 5-8, etc.

Each switch's IP addresses end in whatever their switch number is: fboss1 is always .1 or ::1, fboss 2 is always .2 or ::2, fboss3 is always .3 or ::3.

Each network/port is represented by the second-to-last part of the IP address.
